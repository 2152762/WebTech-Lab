About:

We, the students of Saint Louis University at Baguio City, are advocating for gender equality for those in Brgy. Magsaysay. We advocate because we want to help everyone, especially girls, women and those in the LGBT community, to defend themselves from those who mocked and hurt them just because of their sex, gender and not living up to the societal expectations according to their sex. We also wanted to help the boys and men to not give up on the pressures some people has put on them in order to be called a "real man", not to be ridiculed for their so-called "weaknesses" just because they have emotions and are humans just like women too, and to make everyone to be aware of their problems that have been regarded as problems of the female population only. In short, this advocacy's purpose is help everyone exert their rights as individual human beings who have their own individual differences, do not give in to the pressures of the society, strike the minds of the perpetrators of gender inequality with words, and inform everyone of their sex and gender-related issues that the media has often ignored.
