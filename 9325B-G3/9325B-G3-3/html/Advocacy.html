Overview

The barangay of Magsaysay Private Road implies a strict rule over their cleanliness. Garbage collection is done twice a week, with the segregation of wastes being managed by selected individuals per purok and recyclables being given to volunteers.

Activities


1. There is an operation held once a month by the local government for the cleaning of the creek near the barangay.

2. Kagawad and purok leaders are tasked to manage and maintain cleaniless throughout their jurisdiction.

3. Operation "kalinisan" is help annualy to maintain cleanliness of the barangay.

Organizations

As of now the only present, non-local government and barangay-wide, organization that continuously contribute to the barangays cleanliness is the association of the senior citizens. The barangay is, however, aiming for more involvement of the youth in the future.

Future Plans


1. Promote gardening all throughout the barangay.

2. Possible truck ban or large vehicles in their rodes to reduce congestion of road as well as to lessen local air pollution.

3. Improving on the coherence and proper coordinations of not only the local government but also the residents in the barangay.
